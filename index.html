States of India

Explore the diversity of Indian states

Kerala

Capital: Thiruvananthapuram

Known as "God's Own Country", Kerala is famous for its backwaters, coconut trees, and rich cultural heritage.

Rajasthan

Capital: Jaipur

Known for deserts, palaces, and vibrant folk traditions, Rajasthan offers a glimpse into royal Indian history.

Tamil Nadu

Capital: Chennai

Famous for ancient temples, classical music and dance, and a rich Dravidian culture.

Sikkim

Capital: Gangtok

Located in the Himalayas, Sikkim is known for its beautiful mountains, Buddhist monasteries, and biodiversity.
